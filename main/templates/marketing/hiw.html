How Rallynex Works

Rallynex is built on a simple idea: start a cause, show your progress, and get support. This page explains exactly how you can use the platform, whether you're launching a mission or backing someone else's.

I Want to Start a Cause

1
Create Your Account & Profile
Sign up and create your profile. Tell people who you are and what you care about. You can verify your account with an ID to build extra trust with your supporters.
2
Launch Your Cause
Click "Start a Cause". Give it a clear title, explain your mission, and add photos. You can set an optional funding goal and choose if your cause is public or private to specific followers.
3
Share Progress with Updates
This is the heart of Rallynex. Post regular updates (we call them "Activities") with text, photos, or videos. Share your milestones, challenges, and stories. This builds trust and keeps your community invested in the journey.
4
Receive & Manage Support
As people follow your journey, they can support you. This includes donations, future pledges, or buying related products. You can track everything—funds raised, supporter comments, and engagement—from your simple dashboard.

I Want to Support a Cause

1
Discover Causes You Care About
Browse causes by category or use the search bar. See what causes your friends are supporting. Each cause page shows you the latest progress updates, so you can see the real work being done.
2
Follow the Journey
When you find a cause you believe in, join it. This lets you follow all their updates in one place. You can like and comment on updates to show your encouragement—this emotional support is powerful.
3
Choose How to Support
Support comes in different forms. You can make a one-time donation, make a pledge to give later, or buy a product from the cause. Sometimes, just being an active follower in the comments is the best support you can give.
4
Connect & Stay Updated
Chat directly with cause creators or other supporters. Get notifications when there's a new update or when someone replies to you. You're not just a donor; you're part of the story.

Key Things to Remember:

  • Support is more than money. Liking, commenting, and sharing updates provides crucial emotional backing.
  • Trust is built through progress. Regular, honest updates are what turn one-time visitors into long-term supporters.
  • You're in control. Causes can be public or private. You manage your profile and how you engage.

Ready to Make an Impact?

Whether you're ready to start your own cause or find one to support, Rallynex is the platform built for real, ongoing progress.
