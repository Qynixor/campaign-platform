How It Works

Rallynex is built around one simple principle: start a cause, show your progress, and get support. Here's how both creators and supporters can make the most of the platform.

I Want to Start a Cause

1
Create Your Account & Profile
Sign up, create your profile, and verify your account to build trust. Include details about who you are and what your mission is.
2
Launch Your Cause
Click "Start a Cause", give it a title, describe your mission, and add photos. Optionally, set a funding goal and control privacy settings.
3
Share Progress with Updates
Post text, photos, or videos to share milestones, challenges, and achievements. Regular updates build trust and keep supporters engaged.
4
Receive & Manage Support
Support can be financial (donations or pledges) or non-financial (comments, shares). Track everything from your dashboard: funds raised, supporters, and engagement.

I Want to Support a Cause

1
Discover Causes
Browse by category, use the search bar, or check out featured causes. Read updates to see real progress and impact.
2
Follow the Journey
Join the cause to get all updates. Like and comment to show supportâ€”emotional engagement is just as important as donations.
3
Choose How to Support
Make donations, pledges, or purchase products related to the cause. Active participation in discussions also counts as meaningful support.
4
Connect & Stay Updated
Message cause creators or other supporters. Get notifications on new updates, comments, or replies to your activity.

Key Things to Remember:

  • Support is more than money: Liking, commenting, and sharing updates gives vital encouragement.
  • Trust is built through progress: Regular updates turn visitors into long-term supporters.
  • Control is yours: You manage privacy, profile, and engagement.

Ready to Make an Impact?

Whether you're launching your own cause or supporting someone else's, Rallynex empowers you to make a real difference.
