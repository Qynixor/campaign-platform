Platform Basics

What is Rallynex?
+

Rallynex is an online platform that helps individuals and communities start a cause, share progress through updates, and receive ongoing support. Support can be financial (donations or pledges) or non-financial (community engagement and encouragement).

How do I start a cause on Rallynex?
+

Starting a cause is simple! Click on "Start Your Cause" and follow our guided setup process. You'll be asked to provide details about your cause, set funding goals, and customize your page. You can publish immediately or save as a draft.

What types of causes can I start?
+

Rallynex supports a wide variety of causes including personal projects, community initiatives, creative endeavors, medical expenses, educational goals, business startups, and nonprofit fundraising. As long as your cause is legal and follows our guidelines, you're welcome to start it here.

How much does it cost to use Rallynex?
+

Rallynex is free to start a cause. We charge platform fees only when you receive support: 5% for donations/pledges and 9% for product sales. There are no hidden fees or monthly charges. Payment processing fees may apply separately.

How do I receive funds from my cause?
+

Funds are transferred directly to your bank account or PayPal. You'll need to set up your payout method in your creator dashboard. Transfers typically occur within 3-5 business days after donations are received.
