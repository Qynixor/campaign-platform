Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Rallynex, the platform to start a cause, show progress, and get support.

What is Rallynex?
Rallynex is a platform where you can start a cause, share your progress over time with updates and photos, and receive ongoing support from a community. Support can be financial (donations) or non-financial (community presence and encouragement).
How do I get started?
Sign up for an account and create your profile. To build trust, you can verify your account by submitting ID documents to become a verified user. Once your profile is set up, you can start your first cause.
How do I start a cause?
Click the "Start a Cause" button. Add a title, describe your mission, and set up your page. You can add photos, set an optional funding goal, and choose whether your cause is public or private to specific followers.
What are updates or "Activities"?
Updates (called "Activities") are how you show progress. You can post text, photos, or videos to share milestones, challenges, and stories. This keeps your supporters informed and builds trust in your work.
Can I edit my cause after publishing it?
Yes, you can edit your cause's title, description, and other details anytime from your management dashboard.
How do I track my cause's progress?
Your dashboard shows key metrics like total support received, number of supporters, comments, and engagement on your updates.
What if I don't reach my funding goal?
You keep all funds raised. Setting a goal is optional and meant to inspire support. Your cause can continue to accept support even after reaching or missing its target.
How do I receive donations?
Donations are securely processed through PayPal. You need to add your PayPal email to your profile to receive funds. Once a minimum threshold is met, you can withdraw the money to your PayPal account.
How can I support a cause?
You can support a cause in several ways: by making a donation, committing to a future pledge, purchasing a related product, or simply by joining the cause to follow updates, comment, and show your solidarity.
Do I need to join a cause to support it?
To donate, pledge, or buy products, you need to join the cause first. However, you can like, comment, and see public updates without joining. Joining gives you full access to all activities and deeper engagement.
How do I find causes to support?
Browse by category, use the search bar, explore causes your friends support, or check out featured causes on the homepage. You can mark content as "Not Interested" to improve your recommendations.
What's the difference between a donation and a pledge?
A donation is an immediate financial gift. A pledge is a promise to give a certain amount in the future, which you can fulfill later. Both show incredible support.
How do I connect with others?
You can message any user directly for 1-on-1 chats or create group chats for teams. Use @mentions in comments to tag people. You'll get notifications for new messages, comments, and other interactions.
What are private causes?
Private causes are visible only to the creator and the specific followers they invite. They appear in a separate "Private" tab for those users.
How do I report a problem?
If you see inappropriate content or have a concern, use the "Report" button on the cause page or contact our support team directly through the help section.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes. All financial transactions are handled by secure, third-party payment processors like PayPal. Rallynex does not store your full credit card or bank details.
What are Changemaker Awards?
These are badges that recognize active cause creators:

• Bronze: For starting your first cause.
• Silver: For running multiple causes with good engagement.
• Gold: For creating several high-impact causes.
