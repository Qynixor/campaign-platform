How Auto-Import Works

Once connected, Rallynex will automatically detect new posts from your social accounts and bring them to your Import Queue.

  • 📱 Posts with your journey hashtag appear in the Import Queue
  • 🤖 We'll try to detect the day number from your caption
  • ✅ You can review and assign content before it goes live
  • 🔒 Your data is secure and we never post on your behalf
