How Auto-Import Works

  1. Connect your TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube account in Social Connections
  2. Enable auto-import and set a hashtag for your journey (e.g., #MyJourney)
  3. Post on social media using that hashtag
  4. Your content appears here for review within minutes
  5. Assign to the correct day and approve to publish

Pro tip: Include your journey hashtag in the caption or comments for automatic detection.
