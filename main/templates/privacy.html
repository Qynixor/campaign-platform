Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Information We Collect

When you use Rallynex, we collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information: username, email address, and password
  • Profile information: bio, location, social media usernames
  • Content you create: journey posts, comments, and media uploads
  • Payment information: processed securely through our payment partners

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve Rallynex services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about your account and updates
  • Process payments and donations
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Social Media Integrations

When you connect your TikTok or Instagram account, we access:

  • Your public profile information
  • Posts and media you've shared publicly
  • We never post on your behalf without explicit permission

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us operate Rallynex
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your consent or at your direction

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand usage patterns, and improve Rallynex.

8. Children's Privacy

Rallynex is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or through the platform.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@rallynex.com.
