🏠 General Questions

What is Rallynex?

Rallynex is a journey organizer platform that helps creators structure their content into day-by-day or milestone-based timelines. It's the perfect tool for challenges, learning journeys, startup builds, and fundraising campaigns. 🚀

Is Rallynex free?

Yes! Rallynex is completely free to use for creating and sharing journeys. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core platform will remain free. 💯

Do I need to create an account to follow journeys?

Yes, you need an account to follow journeys and receive notifications. But creating an account is free and takes less than a minute! Signing up also lets you save journeys for later. 📝

🎬 For Creators

How do I create a journey?

Click "Create Journey" from your Dashboard. Fill in your journey title, description, duration, and other details. You can start posting content immediately after creation! 🎯

Can I import content from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, or X/Twitter?

Yes! Our Quick Import feature works with content from any platform. Simply grab the file URL from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, or X/Twitter and paste it into your journey. No account connections, no API setup—just paste and go! 🔗

Just go to your journey's Content Manager, click "Quick Import", paste the URL, and assign it to a day or milestone.

How does fundraising work?

Enable fundraising when creating or editing your journey. Set a goal amount and description. Supporters can donate via PayPal. Funds are transferred directly to your connected PayPal account. 💰

What types of journeys can I create?

You can create Daily Challenge journeys (post content every day) or Milestone journeys (post key achievements). Choose from templates like Fitness, Portfolio, or Startup to match your content style. 🎨

Can I edit my journey after publishing?

Absolutely! You can edit your journey title, description, cover image, and settings anytime from your Dashboard > My Journeys > Edit. You can also add, edit, or remove content posts at any time. ✏️

💝 For Supporters

How do I follow a journey?

Visit any journey page and click the "Follow" button. You'll receive notifications when new content is posted. You can manage your followed journeys from your Dashboard. 🔔

Is my donation secure?

Yes! All donations are processed through PayPal, a trusted global payment provider. Rallynex does not store your payment information. Your financial data stays with PayPal's secure platform. 🔒

Can I support multiple journeys?

Absolutely! You can follow and support as many journeys as you want. Each journey is independent, so you can support all the creators and causes you care about. ❤️

How do I share a journey with others?

Every journey has its own unique URL. Just copy the link from your browser's address bar and share it anywhere—social media, email, messaging apps, or your bio. 📎
