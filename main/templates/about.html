Build in Public. Document Your Journey.

Rallynex is the platform where builders, founders, and creators document their journey — structured, shareable, and built for accountability.

✨ Our Mission

Most building happens in the dark. Rallynex makes it public — turning daily progress into a timeline that builds trust, attracts followers, and proves the work behind every product.

Daily Logs

Document what you built, learned, or struggled with every day. Your journey, day by day.

Product Tracking

Track your product stage from idea to MVP, beta, launch, and beyond. Show your progress.

Build Community

Followers, comments, and encouragement around every milestone you share. Build your audience.

Progress Analytics

Track views, followers, and engagement. Know what resonates with your audience.

Reflections

Learn from your journey. Document lessons, mistakes, and moments of clarity.

One Link

Every journey gets a unique URL. Share it anywhere — bio, email, social, anywhere.

📖 Why Build in Public

Building in public changes everything. It creates accountability, attracts early adopters, and builds trust before you even launch. Every day you share your progress, you invite people into your story.

But most builders struggle to stay consistent. They post sporadically across different platforms. Their progress is scattered and hard to follow. The timeline gets lost.

Rallynex solves that. A single timeline where every step is visible, every milestone is documented, and your progress becomes the content. No more scattered updates. No more losing your journey.

Built solo. Bootstrapped. No outside funding — just conviction that building in public changes how products are built and communities are formed.
