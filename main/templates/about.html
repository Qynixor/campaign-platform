{# Story #}

Why I Built Rallynex

In my country, I've watched too many young people rally behind wealthy individuals on social media—men and women claiming to run NGOs, collecting serious money from supporters. But in reality, most own no NGO at all. They're scamming people who genuinely want to help.

I searched the internet for a tool where honest, hardworking individuals running real causes could showcase their work transparently. Something where supporters could click a link in a profile and see the entire journey unfold—proof that the work is real. I found nothing.

So I built Rallynex. Alone. No team, no funding—just a belief that transparency can stop the scamming. With Rallynex, real creators and organizations can document their journey day by day. Supporters can watch the progress, comment, and trust what they see. No more blind faith. No more stolen money. Just verified, visible work.

This is just the beginning. Every journey shared on Rallynex is one step closer to a world where trust is built on proof, not promises.
