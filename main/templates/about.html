Why This Exists

The internet celebrates results. Finished products. Launch days. Before-and-after photos. But the months between — the iterations, setbacks, and daily discipline — remain invisible.

That gap matters. Trust is built in the process, not the announcement. Followers invest in people they've watched grow, not static profiles.

Rallynex gives that process a structure. A single timeline where every step is visible, every milestone is documented, and progress becomes the content.

Built solo. Bootstrapped. No outside funding — just conviction that transparency changes how people build online.
